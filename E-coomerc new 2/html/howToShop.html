How to shop

How to shop online on our site ?

  1. By clicking on the creat nov button, you create a new account, register!
  2. Click on the login in the login and thus enter the login of your username and password
  3. By entering username and password data, you access our online store
  4. We select the quantities you go to the addtocard and the team enters the selected product in your cart
  5. Select the quantity, go to addToCard and then enter the selected product in your cart
  6. In the basket you can remove, look at product prices and quantity!
  7. By clicking on nex you will choose which way you want to pay !
  8. The last thing you need to do is go to the order icon and you have ordered our product!
  9. Note: the data you enter during registration must be entered correctly, in order for delivery to be delivered properly!
